LETTER TEMPLATE: Initial Prior Authorization — Spinal Cord Stimulator Implantation
Template ID: TMPL-IA-SCS-001
Service Category: pain_management
Letter Type: initial_auth

INSTRUCTIONS:
Generate a prior authorization letter for spinal cord stimulator permanent implantation.
Must include psychological evaluation results, SCS trial outcomes (>= 50% relief), and
comprehensive multimodal treatment failure documentation. This is a high-complexity
authorization requiring extensive documentation.

TEMPLATE STRUCTURE:

[Today's Date]

[Payer Name]
Attn: Medical Director
[Payer Address]

RE: Prior Authorization Request — Spinal Cord Stimulator Implantation
Patient: [Patient Full Name]
Date of Birth: [DOB]
Member ID: [Member ID]

Dear Medical Director,

I am requesting prior authorization for permanent spinal cord stimulator (SCS)
implantation (CPT [CPT Codes]) for [Patient Name], who has been diagnosed with
chronic intractable pain secondary to [Primary Diagnosis].

CHRONIC PAIN HISTORY
[Duration of chronic pain condition. Etiology and pathophysiology. Prior surgical
history if applicable (e.g., failed back surgery syndrome — procedure type, date,
facility, surgeon). Progression of symptoms over time. Current pain levels (VAS/NRS).
Impact on activities of daily living, employment, sleep, and psychosocial functioning.]

MULTIMODAL TREATMENT HISTORY
[Comprehensive list of ALL treatments attempted, organized by modality:

Physical Therapy:
- Program dates, number of visits, specific protocols used
- Functional outcomes and reason for discontinuation

Pharmacotherapy:
- Each medication: name, dose, duration, response, side effects
- Include NSAIDs, neuropathic agents, muscle relaxants, opioids, topicals
- Document rational for medication changes

Interventional Procedures:
- Each procedure: type, date, approach, response (% relief, duration)
- Include ESIs, nerve blocks, facet injections, medial branch blocks, RFA
- Document diminishing returns or failure patterns

Behavioral/Psychological Interventions:
- Cognitive behavioral therapy, biofeedback, pain psychology
- Dates, duration, outcomes]

PSYCHOLOGICAL EVALUATION
[Evaluator name, credentials, and date of evaluation. Key findings:
- Cognitive functioning
- Mood assessment (depression, anxiety screening results)
- Coping mechanisms and pain catastrophizing scores
- Substance abuse screening results
- Recommendation regarding SCS candidacy
- Confirmation of no untreated psychiatric contraindications
- Realistic expectations assessment]

SCS TRIAL RESULTS
[Trial dates (start to end, minimum 5-7 days). Device manufacturer and model.
Lead placement (spinal level, electrode configuration). Daily pain logs showing:
- Pre-trial baseline pain level
- Daily pain levels during trial
- Percent pain relief achieved (must be >= 50%)
Functional improvement metrics:
- Walking distance/tolerance
- Sitting tolerance
- Sleep quality
- Activity participation
Patient satisfaction assessment.
Any adverse events during trial.]

MEDICAL NECESSITY
[Point-by-point alignment with payer policy criteria. Clinical rationale for
permanent implantation based on successful trial. Expected long-term outcomes.
References to clinical guidelines (NACC, APS) and published literature supporting
SCS for this indication. Why the patient is not a candidate for further corrective
surgery.]
