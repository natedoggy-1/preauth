LETTER TEMPLATE: Initial Prior Authorization — Interventional Pain Management
Template ID: TMPL-IA-PAIN-001
Service Category: pain_management
Letter Type: initial_auth

INSTRUCTIONS:
Generate a prior authorization letter for an interventional pain procedure (ESI,
nerve block, facet injection, etc.). Include specific imaging findings, prior
injection response data, and medication trial outcomes. Address each payer criterion
individually.

DIAGNOSIS AND CLINICAL PRESENTATION
[Primary and secondary diagnoses with ICD-10 codes. Detailed symptom description
including pain location, radiation pattern, intensity (VAS/NRS), duration, and

sensory testing, reflex changes, provocative tests (SLR, Spurling).]

DIAGNOSTIC IMAGING
[MRI or CT findings with specific structural pathology identified. Correlation
between imaging findings and clinical presentation. Include imaging date, modality,
and key measurements.]

PRIOR CONSERVATIVE TREATMENT
[Medication trials: each medication with dose, duration, and outcome
Physical therapy: program details, number of visits, functional outcomes
Prior injection procedures (if any): type, date, approach, guidance method,

MEDICAL NECESSITY JUSTIFICATION
[Clinical rationale for the requested procedure. Alignment with evidence-based
guidelines. Expected therapeutic benefit. Why conservative measures have been

PROCEDURE PLAN
[Specific procedure requested, anatomic target (spinal level, laterality),
approach (transforaminal, interlaminar, caudal), image guidance method
(fluoroscopy, CT), medications to be injected (steroid type and dose,
local anesthetic).]
