Cigna Medical Coverage Policy: Spinal Cord Stimulator (SCS) Implantation
Policy Number: 0112 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024

POLICY SUMMARY
Cigna considers spinal cord stimulator implantation medically necessary for patients
with chronic intractable pain who have failed comprehensive multimodal conservative
treatment and demonstrated successful response during an SCS trial period.

CLINICAL CRITERIA â€” ALL of the following must be met:

1. QUALIFYING DIAGNOSIS
   Chronic intractable pain secondary to one of the following:
   - Failed back surgery syndrome (postlaminectomy syndrome, M96.1)
   - Complex regional pain syndrome (CRPS, G90.5x)
   - Chronic painful peripheral neuropathy
   Pain must be primarily neuropathic in character.

2. PAIN DURATION
   Pain duration >= 6 months with documented failure of multimodal conservative care
   including physical therapy, pharmacotherapy, psychological interventions, and
   interventional procedures.

3. PSYCHOLOGICAL EVALUATION
   Comprehensive psychological evaluation completed within 12 months of the SCS
   request by a licensed psychologist or psychiatrist. Evaluation must confirm:
   - No untreated psychiatric contraindication to implantation
   - Appropriate coping skills and realistic expectations
   - Absence of secondary gain issues

4. SUCCESSFUL SCS TRIAL
   Successful SCS trial period meeting ALL of the following:
   - Trial duration of 5-7 days minimum
   - >= 50% pain relief during the trial period
   - Documented functional improvement (activities, mobility, sleep)
   - Patient satisfaction with trial results

5. SUBSTANCE ABUSE SCREENING
   No active substance abuse disorder, or documented completion of a substance
   abuse treatment program with ongoing monitoring.

6. SURGICAL CANDIDACY
   Patient is not a candidate for further corrective surgery that would address
   the underlying pain generator.

7. FOLLOW-UP PLAN
   Documented follow-up plan including:
   - Device programming and management schedule
   - Physical therapy post-implantation
   - Medication tapering plan (if applicable)
   - Regular follow-up visits

REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION:
- Psychological evaluation report
- SCS trial procedure notes and daily pain logs
- Physical therapy records
- Comprehensive medication history
- Imaging studies
- Prior surgical records (if applicable)
